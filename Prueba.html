What is ADHC

Adult Day Health Care (ADHC) is a community-based, long-term care program that is true alternative to a nursing home. Services are designed to provide social and medical assistance to adults who need supervised care in a safe place outside their home during the day. Adult day centers generally operate during normal business hours five to six days a week
